Our History

Since 1952, The Original Henry's has been a cornerstone of San Angelo's culinary scene. Founded by Henry Hogeda, a passionate chef with a dream to bring authentic Mexican flavors to Texas, our restaurant has grown into a beloved local institution.

Henry's vision was simple: to create a place where families could gather, share delicious meals, and celebrate life. With recipes passed down through generations and a commitment to using the freshest ingredients, Henry built a legacy that continues to thrive today.

From our famous tacos to our hearty enchiladas, every dish is a tribute to Henry's passion for authentic Mexican cuisine. We invite you to join us and be a part of our story as we continue to serve the flavors of Mexico with pride and passion.
